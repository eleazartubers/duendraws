About Me

Short Introduction

Ms. Leizyl Duen Cruz is a talented digital artist specializing in digital painting and illustration with a passion for creating a character in a fiction book using her imagination. She began her artistic journey in the year 2020, the pandemic year, while she studied for her college degree her drawing served as an extra income that helped her studies and the family. She graduated “MAGNA CUM LAUDE” with a Bachelor of Business Technology and Livelihood Education Major in Industrial Arts at the Polytechnic University of the Philippines.

Her drawing inspiration comes from a famous Wattpad Author who owns the name "Jonaxx.” Her work and dedication to every story inspire Ms. Leizyl to do her artwork. She has been a fan of the fictional story of Jonaxx since 2016 and repeats the story to create more artwork using her imagination.
